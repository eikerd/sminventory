The "Smart" Spreadsheet Layout
The view is shifted from a grid to a high-density table with a focus on visual health indicators.
1. Header & Filter Bar
Search: Stays pinned at the top left.
Quick Filters: Add pill-shaped toggles next to the search: [All] [Missing Models] [Not Scanned] [Ready to Run].
Global Actions: "Scan All" and "Download Missing" buttons moved to the top right.

4. Smart Functional Improvements
Conditional Formatting: If a workflow has "Missing Models," the text for that row turns slightly more amber. If "Ready to Run," the name turns bright white.
Inline Scan: Clicking the "Scan" icon triggers a small loading spinner inside the cell, updating the "Health" and "Dependencies" columns in real-time without refreshing the page.
Expandable Rows: Clicking the workflow name drops down a small sub-panel listing every required model and its individual status (Found / Missing / Downloading).
Sticky Header: The column headers stay at the top as you scroll through your 24+ workflows.
